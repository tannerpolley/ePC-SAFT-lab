# Source Assets

- `data/reference/multiphase/ascani_case2_model_comparison.csv` records the local source-backed Case 2 feed, paper phase-composition targets, historical model comparison values, charge residuals, material balance residual, and thermodynamic diagnostics.
- `docs/papers/md/Ascani, Sadowski, Held - 2022 - Calculation of Multiphase Equilibria Containing Mixed Solvents and M.md` is the local markdown extraction of the paper.
- `docs/papers/md/Ascani, Sadowski, Held - 2022 - Supporting Information for Calculation of multiphase equilibria containing mixed solvents and mixed..md` is the local supporting-information markdown extraction.

The validation target is the mixed-solvent electrolyte LLE Case 2 feed containing water, butanol, NaCl, and KCl at 298.15 K.
